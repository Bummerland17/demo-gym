About Us

Built by Athletes, for Athletes

Iron Peak CrossFit was founded in 2018 by Coach Marcus Webb — a 10-year CrossFit veteran and former Division I athlete. We built this gym because we believed Phoenix deserved a box with serious coaching and a real community.

We're not a globo gym. Everyone knows your name here. Every class, every rep counts.
